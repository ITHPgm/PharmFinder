How accurate is the "Open Now" status?
We update pharmacy hours regularly and cross-reference with user reports. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend calling ahead for critical needs.

No! PharmFinder works instantly without any sign-up or login. Just open the app, allow location access, and find pharmacies near you.

Your location is used only in real-time to find nearby pharmacies and is never stored or shared with third parties. Privacy is a core commitment.

PharmFinder is built to work locally in any country. We are expanding coverage continuously, starting with major cities across Africa, and growing globally.
