Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

How accurate is the "Open Now" status?
We update pharmacy hours regularly and cross-reference with user reports. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend calling ahead for critical needs.
Do I need to create an account?
No! PharmFinder works instantly without any sign-up or login. Just open the app, allow location access, and find pharmacies near you. However, pharmacy owners need an account to list their business.
Is my location data stored or shared?
Your location is used only in real-time to find nearby pharmacies and is never stored or shared with third parties. Privacy is a core commitment.
How can I list my pharmacy on PharmFinder?
Click the "List Your Pharmacy" button above or register an account. Once verified, you can add your pharmacy details, hours, and contact information for free.
